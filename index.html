Controls


  • Move:    Use   W, A, S, D   to move.

  • Attack:   Use   I  to cast a spell.
  •           Use   P   to attack Voldemort.

Health

  • Lose Lives:
    • Draco Malfoy will reduce your health by 1.
    • Severus Snape will reduce your health by 1.
    • Getting caught by Lord Voldemort ends your game.
    • The Bludger will reduce your health by 1 if it hits you.

Objectives

  • Score Points:
    • Catch the Snitch to earn 1 point.
    • Hit an enemy with a spell to earn 1 point.
    • Hit Voldemort to earn 5 points
